E-Manager

This individual project developed by her is an ultimate guide for Electricity Management which would help companies monitor and control consumption while keeping usage levels at its minimum. The presence of a predictor in this web application enables the user to predict the consumption and cost in the following months analyzing past data.

Project Temp-O-Rent

A vehicle rental application for the Sri Lankan consumer base which involves two customer categories; vehicle provider and borrower. This project was developed by a team of IIT undergraduates in 2020 (February). Unique functionalities in the system include comprehensive vehicle insurance, roadside assistance, secure lockbox fit to vehicle and continuous tracking.

Project Healthy Eats

A Mobile Application developed by team Phoenix which provides a variety of new recipes, mainly targeting individuals with awful illnesses such as diabetics, cholesterol and high blood pressure. It collects data from the user about the diseases they have and level of the disease where meal plans are generated to suit the requirements. The user can also select among a wide variety of recipes according to his/her preferences based on their health conditions.
